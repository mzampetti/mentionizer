Mentionizer

It's good Twitter Etiquette™ to mention the person who wrote an article when tweeting about it!

Mentionizer helps you quickly discover the twitter handle of whoever wrote the blog post you're reading, so that you don't have to scavenge for information on the page.

To use the bookmarklet just drag the Mentionizer button to your Bookmarks Bar, browse for a page you like and then click it.
